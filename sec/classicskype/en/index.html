Why is this site needed?

This site was created by those who are not indifferent to Skype and its older versions even in 2024.

In 2018, support for older versions of Skype (7.x or lower) was discontinued, which not everyone liked. The new Skype 8 on the Electron engine had a rather poor design at first, used many more resources compared to 7.x, Skype 8 lacks many features previously available in older versions...

However, caring users, 6 years later, are still looking for ways to return the old client because of its convenience, design, sounds...

This project is trying to help these users!
What can you do on this site?
Get the latest news about Classic Skype
Install mods of 8.x versions with old sounds for Windows and Android
Explore old versions, their structure, interface
Find users by interests
Join group discussions
Get answers to your top questions about Classic Skype
