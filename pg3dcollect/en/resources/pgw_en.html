On the "Pixel Gun X" server, which I hate, well, not the point, a man like RobDEV returned. It was he who, in 2022, made the revival of PGW 1.5.2 and the port of PG3D 4.7, which later grew into Pixel Gun Infinity. In May 2022, he left the community of the old pg due to disagreements with other administrators of the old pg. In January 2023, RobDEV posted screenshots of his PS Vita game on his Reddit account. Already on the screenshot, you could see a bunch of PGW assets: interface, maps, dynamic objects. And in June 2023, Rob went back to "Pixel Gun X". And immediately Rob presented his development: he was able to revive the PGW. So far, the project is in closed beta, but the release is expected on June 23, 2023.
